Poems Interesting Facts

I have not yet held your hand,
Nor walked with you across the sand.
But in my heart, you’re already near,
A gentle voice I long to hear.

I dream of you in quiet nights,
A star that shines with endless light.
Though I have not yet seen your face,
I feel your warmth, your gentle grace.

We stand apart, two souls unknown,
Yet love has found a way to grow.
No stories shared, no past to bind,
But still you linger in my mind.

I do not know the path you take,
Or how your mornings gently wake.
But still I feel you close to me,
A love that waits patiently.

I write to you with hope and care,
Though I don’t know the clothes you wear.
I’ve never seen your gentle eyes,
But still I dream beneath the skies.

I wonder how your laughter feels,
What stories all your silence seals.
Though I have not yet heard your voice,
My heart has made its tender choice.

I’ve never touched your gentle hand,
Or walked with you across the land.
But still I feel your spirit near,
A quiet love that’s crystal clear.

I do not know the sound you make,
Or how your mornings softly wake.
But still I dream of you each night,
A gentle flame, a guiding light.

I wonder where your footsteps fall,
What dreams you chase, what hopes you call.
Though I have not yet seen your face,
I feel your warmth, your gentle grace.

I’ve never heard your gentle tone,
Or walked with you when day is done.
But still I feel you close to me,
A love that waits patiently.

Forever yours, Nwokedi
